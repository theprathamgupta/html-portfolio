My top 3 movies of all-time

The Dark Knight (2008)

This film is almost universally lauded and often cited as not just one of the best superhero movies, but one of the greatest films of its era.

Superman (1978)

Richard Donner's original "Superman: The Movie" captivated audiences and made them "believe a man could fly.

Wonder Woman (2017)

This film was a critical and commercial success, finally giving one of DC's most iconic heroes a strong, standalone live-action movie.
